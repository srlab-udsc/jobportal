About the Lab:
We are part of the Plant Molecular Biology Department at UDSC, where our research is dedicated to exploring the molecular mechanisms underlying plant growth and development. Our primary focus areas include the study of microRNAs (miRNAs) in gene regulation, advancements in rice genomics and breeding, and the integration of bioinformatics approaches to accelerate discovery in plant science. The lab brings together interdisciplinary expertise to address key challenges in crop improvement and plant biology.
